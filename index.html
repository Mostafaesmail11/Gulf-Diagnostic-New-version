Our services

Complete Blood Work & Biochemistry

CBC, liver & kidney function, electrolytes and inflammatory markers — processed on-site. Same-day results, major insurance accepted.

Cholesterol & Lipid Profile

Total cholesterol, HDL, LDL and triglycerides, reviewed against your age, medications and risk factors.

Hormone & Thyroid Panel

TSH, T3, T4, reproductive hormones, vitamin D and B12 — panels for fatigue, weight change and cycle concerns.

Diabetes & HbA1c Monitoring

Fasting glucose, HbA1c and glucose tolerance — the full diabetes picture on your consultant's schedule.

General Medical Consultation

Consultation with Dr. Sami or Dr. Rubia to interpret results, adjust medication and plan next steps.

Pre-Employment & Wellness Screening

Fit-to-work screenings, annual check-ups and bundled wellness packages for individuals and employers.
